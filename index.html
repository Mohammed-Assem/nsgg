ABOUT US

Nasser Scouts Groups started as a community initiative in downtown Qalyubia, aiming to help youth discover their potential through real-world adventures, civic leadership, and collaborative teamwork. It soon became obvious that our mission could expand beyond typical outdoor survival to truly nurture foundational leadership skills from the very get-go.

Currently, we offer scout training, community development projects, and outdoor exploration frameworks in order to build character, resilience, and a lasting spirit of service. We value our core scout laws above everything else, meaning that we never stop short of guiding individuals to give their absolute best effort.
